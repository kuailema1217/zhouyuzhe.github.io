Welcome to Energy Lab

Our research is based on the data provided by Sustainable Engineering Lab at Columbia University.
This is a platform providing an overall introduction of the collected data and some methods to analyze the data.

Introduction

Data is comprised of energy demand and environmental factors over several years in 24 cities. The data is displayed in year-month-day pattern.

Prediction

Two methods in predicting peak demand:
1.Time series method; 2.Defining "similarity" coefficient between cities to find the most "close" city pairs for prediction. And their comparison.

Value at Risk

Analogy to the concept in Finance. Focus on the tail behavior. Look for the intraday 24 hour demands value under a certain "risk".
